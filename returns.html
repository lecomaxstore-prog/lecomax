Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with your order, you can return it within 30 days of receipt.

  • Tags and seals must be intact.

Exchange Process

To exchange an item for a different size or color, please follow the return process and verify availability with our support team.

Once we receive your return, we will inspect it and notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
