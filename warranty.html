Warranty Policy

Coverage for your peace of mind.

1-Year Standard Warranty

All electronic devices purchased from Lecomax come with a 1-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects.

What is Covered?

  • Hardware failures under normal use.
  • Defective materials or workmanship.
  • Battery issues (if below 80% capacity within 6 months).

What is NOT Covered?

  • Accidental damage (drops, spills, cracks).
  • Cosmetic damage (scratches, dents).
  • Modifications or unauthorized repairs.
How to claim warranty

Contact us via WhatsApp at +212 699-289568 with your order receipt and a description of the issue. We will arrange for inspection and repair or replacement.
