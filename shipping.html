Shipping Information

Fast, reliable delivery across Morocco.

Delivery Time

We strive to deliver your order as quickly as possible. Typical delivery times are:

  • Casablanca & Rabat: 24 hours
  • Major Cities (Agadir, Tangier, Marrakech, Fez, etc.): 24-48 hours
  • Remote Areas: 2-4 business days

Shipping Costs

We offer competitive shipping rates for all orders.

  • Standard Shipping: 35 MAD
  • Free Shipping on orders over 500 MAD

Payment Methods

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD). You pay only when you receive your package and verify your items.

Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, our support team will keep you updated via WhatsApp. You can also contact us at +212 699-289568 for real-time status updates.
